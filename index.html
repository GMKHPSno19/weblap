About us



Government Model Kannada Higher Primary School No.19 , is a higher primary school with top quality education for students from standards 1-8, with highly interactive and friendly staff your child will be nurtured in an ideal atmosphere that will play a vital role in shaping his future.The school also has facilities including library, science lab and much more, providing them full fledged education as well as practical knowledge. Various cultural activities are also conducted ensuring holistic development of the students. Physical education is provided to the students as well, ensuring that the students are physically fit.
